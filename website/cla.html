  • Submitting a PR = agreeing to these terms

Why a CLA?

envpod is dual-licensed:

  • BSL 1.1 — free for everyone to use, study, modify, and distribute
  • Commercial license — for companies that want to embed envpod in closed-source products or offer it as a managed service without BSL obligations

To maintain the ability to offer commercial licenses, we need permission from all contributors to license their contributions under both the BSL 1.1 and the commercial license. Without this agreement, outside contributions could only be distributed under BSL 1.1, which would prevent us from offering the commercial license that funds development.

This is standard practice for dual-licensed open source projects. Qt, MongoDB, GitLab, and many others use the same model.
